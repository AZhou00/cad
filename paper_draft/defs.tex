\section*{Parameter Definitions}

\begin{tabular}{ll}
$s = 1, \ldots, N_{\text{scan}}$ & Scan index \\
$t$ & Time index within a scan \\
$p = 1, \ldots, N_{\text{pix}}$ & Pixel index on the sky \\
$\alpha, \beta$ & Detector index \\
$\mathbf{d}_s$ & Time-ordered data (TOD) for scan $s$ \\
$P$ & Pointing matrix mapping pixels to TOD \\
$L_s$ & Wind advection operator for scan $s$ \\
$\mathbf{c}$ & Static CMB field, defined on pixels \\
$\mathbf{a}_s^0$ & Atmospheric field at $t=0$ for scan $s$ \\
$\mathbf{w}_s$ & Wind vector (deg/s) for scan $s$ \\
$\boldsymbol{\epsilon}_s$ & Instrumental noise (Gaussian, white) \\
$C_a(\theta)$ & Atmospheric 2-point correlation function \\
$C_{\rm CMB}(\theta)$ & CMB 2-point correlation function \\
$C_a, C_c$ & Pixel-space covariance matrices for atmosphere and CMB \\
$\tilde{N}_s$ & Effective per-scan covariance after atmosphere marginalization
\end{tabular}
